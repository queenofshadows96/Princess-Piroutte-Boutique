# Princess Pirouette Boutique Website Requirements

## Design & Branding

- Primary color scheme:
  - Light gold: `#D4AF37`
  - Dark gold: `#B8860B`
  - Pink accent: `#FFD1DC`
  - Background: `#FFF5F7`
  - Body text: `#C09090`

- Typography:
  - Decorative title font: `Great Vibes` (referred to as `magnolia` in code).
  - Heading font: `Playfair Display` italic.

- General UI style:
  - No emojis in the UI.
  - Use elegant SVG graphics instead of emojis.

## Layout & Components

- Navbar:
  - Links: Home, Shop, Sizing Guide, FAQ, About, Our Values, Contact.
  - Includes a cart icon.

- Card styling:
  - Border: `2px solid #B8860B`.
  - Background: white.

- Floating stars background animation must be present.

## Integrations

- Data backend: Supabase for products and orders.
- Payments: Stripe.
- Contact form: EmailJS.

## Pages

The website must include the following pages:

- Home
- Shop
- Shop/[id]
- About
- FAQ
- Sizing Guide
- Our Values
- Contact
- Checkout
- Checkout/Success
- Checkout/Cancel
